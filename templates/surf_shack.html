About This Space

Your Perfect Beach Escape

Welcome to the Surf Shack — a new build just minutes from all of Panama City's top attractions. Have fun in the loft arcade hangout space, or head outside to a backyard oasis with a covered patio, mini golf, cornhole, workout equipment, fire pit, loungers, and outdoor shower.

Fire up the electric BBQ grill, kick back in the hammock, or soak up the sun. Inside, relax on the comfy sofa with Smart TV. Sleep in the King master bedroom — plus a Queen trundle in the loft.

Beach towels, umbrella, snorkeling gear, and all the essentials are provided. Everything you need for the perfect Gulf Coast getaway.
