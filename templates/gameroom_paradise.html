About This Space

Your Louisiana Getaway

This getaway guesthouse has 1 bedroom and 1 bath, centrally located between the Cajun swamps, the festive city of New Orleans, and the airport. Sleeps up to 4 guests comfortably — sleeping arrangements include a queen bed, a full bed, a sofa, and an air mattress.

Entertainment includes foosball, pool table, pinball, and darts inside — plus a swimming pool, covered patio, and private backyard outside. Come experience a true taste of Louisiana: festivals, Creole cuisine, annual celebrations, and unique dialects.

This is a private standalone guesthouse. Guests have full access to the entire guesthouse, swimming pool, patio, and backyard. The property owner lives nearby in the main house — you'll have your own private space.
