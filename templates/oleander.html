About This Space

Fort Worth's City Central Hideout

A new build central hideout minutes from downtown Fort Worth, the Stockyards, and TCU. Enjoy endless fun in the gameroom with foosball, arcade, basketball, mini golf, and a full gaming setup.

Step out to a patio turf paradise with fire pit, sun loungers, and BBQ. Stay fit in the private garage home gym. Inside, relax on the spacious sofa with Smart TV in every room.

Sleep in a spacious King bedroom with sofa sleeper, plus Queen and Full bunk rooms — single-level, no stairs, in a peaceful up-and-coming neighborhood.

✈️ Host offers rideshare to/from Dallas Love Field & DFW Airport — ask about availability when booking.
